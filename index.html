Induction Week Hackathon--Team 22

We choose the theme *Children in Harms Way* and explore the solution pathway: A Community Voice!
We expect to create a safety/emergency Service system that is built into various smart devices for children and an APP for parents to change settings of our services.
Our greatest innovation is connecting to all the members and digital devices of the community so that anything close to the children in need can help!
