Induction Week Hackathon--Team 22

We choose the theme *Children in Harms Way* and explore the solution pathway: A Community Voice!
Our solution is an app designed to be used by caregivers and children. It is intended to be used as an emergency signal if a child is thought to be in danger by algorithms which utilise a smartphone/watch's built in sensors .
Our greatest innovation is connecting to all members and digital devices of the community so that anything close to the children in need can help!
